AI for Documentation & Reporting

Auto-generate project charters, status reports, risk registers, change logs, and lessons-learned documents. Reduce report prep from 45 minutes to 5.

PMI Alignment

Process Groups: Initiating, Planning, Monitoring & Controlling, Closing Knowledge Areas: Integration, Scope, Communications, Quality, Risk

5-Step Implementation Guide

Audit Existing Document Templates

Inventory every PM artifact your org uses — charter, scope statement, WBS, risk register, status report, change log, lessons learned. Rate each by effort-to-produce (high = AI candidate). Prioritize the top 5.

PMI: Integration → Develop Project Management Plan

Create AI Prompt Templates per Document

For each artifact, build a reusable prompt template with placeholders. Include your org's format, tone, and required sections. Store in a shared Notion/Confluence page for team reuse.

PMI: Quality → Plan Quality Management · Tools: Claude, Notion AI

Connect Data Sources for Auto-Population

Link Jira/Asana metrics, timesheets, budget data, and risk logs to your AI pipeline. The more real-time data AI has, the less manual input you need. Use Zapier or direct API for connections.

PMI: Integration → Monitor & Control Project Work · Tools: Zapier, Make

Generate, Review, Refine

AI generates the first draft (80% done). PM reviews for accuracy, adds judgment-based commentary (stakeholder sentiment, strategic context), and polishes. Total time: 5-10 min vs. 45-60 min manually.

PMI: Communications → Manage Communications

Archive & Build Knowledge Base

Store all generated documents with metadata (project, phase, date). AI can later query this knowledge base to improve future estimates, identify recurring risks, and build organizational process assets (OPAs).

PMI: Closing → Close Project or Phase · Tools: Confluence AI, Notion AI

Risk Register Creation & Update

Planning → Risk Management

Describe the project context and AI generates a comprehensive risk register with risk ID, category, description, probability, impact, risk score, owner, and mitigation strategy for each identified risk.

RiskQuality
Project context: E-commerce platform migration from monolith to microservices. 6-month timeline, team of 12, 3 external vendor dependencies. Generate a risk register with 10 risks. For each: - Risk ID, Category (Technical/External/Organizational) - Description, Probability (1-5), Impact (1-5) - Risk Score (P x I), Risk Owner - Mitigation Strategy, Contingency Plan
